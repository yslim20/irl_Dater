nimbus

as team nimbus, we were able to come together and create this delightful dating app. when we first met, we all noticed we enjoyed video games. so we took inspiration from dating simulation games, and the idea of irl_Dater came to life.

irl_Dater is meant to help single people meet others in a fun atmosphere. the game aspect of this dating app will allow users to have an exciting time while building meaningful relationships.

we had a great time creating irl_Dater, and we hope you enjoy finding your true love!
